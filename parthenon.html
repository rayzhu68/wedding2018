OUR STORY

HOW WE MET

Yubing and Ken met at a bike shop on the Tsinghua University campus in Beijing on a sunny day in October 2011. Yubing was with a girlfriend and Ken was with some guy friends. They all got chatting. That's when Yubing and Ken discovered their similarities...

FIRST DATE(S)

Let's just say, first date No. 1 was not a resounding success. Not only was Yubing 2 hours late but she didn't even realise it was a date :P so smooth operator Ken quickly shifted gears and moved the "date" location from a cute café to a local bar and grabbed a couple of friends so when Yubing arrived, he was casually playing pool, not even noticing she was late (!). First date No. 2 went much better. Having told Yubing he knew all the best places to go out (even on a Tuesday) Yubing called his bluff and suggested they go out (on Tuesday). As a man who always delivers, Ken rounded up the troops and they had an epic night out on the times - shapes were made in Coco Banana! Many karaoke sessions and many more meals later, the love birds flew back to London...
